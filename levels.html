Tips:

  • Be wise with using the sword! You only have a limited count of attacks!
  • Keep away from spider webs -- they can slow you down
  • Reach diamonds when you can -- they make you sprint fast!
  • Keep an eye on the mini map in the top-right corner to see your position and enemy locations. Click on the mini map to expand it for a better view

GOOD LUCK, and may you survive the maze
